Vendor Merchant Agreement

For Sellers & Service Providers

Welcome to Bayna.

This Vendor Merchant Agreement ("Agreement") is a legally binding contract between you ("Vendor", "Seller", "Service Provider", "you", "your") and Bayna ("Company", "we", "us", "our") governing your participation as a vendor on the Bayna marketplace platform. By registering as a vendor or service provider, you agree to comply with all terms set forth in this Agreement.

1. Onboarding & Verification

To list products or services on Bayna, you must complete our verification process. This process is designed to maintain trust and safety within our marketplace community.

Verification Requirements:

  • Valid government-issued identification (National ID, Driver's License, or Passport)
  • Business registration documents (for formal businesses)
  • Business address and contact information
  • Bank account or Mobile Money details for payouts
  • Product or service category verification

Verification Timeline: Standard verification typically takes 1-3 business days. Expedited verification may be available for qualified businesses.

Bayna reserves the right to reject verification applications or suspend verified status for vendors who fail to maintain compliance with this Agreement.

2. Pricing Transparency

Prohibited Practice: The use of "Price in DM" (Direct Message) tactics is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account suspension or termination.

Vendors agree to list the total, all-inclusive price of goods or services directly on their product or service listings. Price transparency requirements include:

  • Displaying the full price upfront, including all fees and charges
  • No hidden costs or unexpected fees at checkout
  • Clear indication of any discounts or promotional pricing
  • Accurate pricing for all product variations (sizes, colors, quantities)
  • Immediate price updates when changes occur

Why This Matters: Price transparency builds trust with customers and ensures a fair marketplace for all vendors. Violations of this policy damage the integrity of the platform.

3. Payments & Payouts

Payment Processing: Payments made through the Bayna platform are processed via secure payment gateways including:

  • Orange Money
  • Africell Money
  • Other approved Mobile Money providers
  • Bank transfers (where available)

Payout Schedule: Payouts to vendors are processed according to the following schedule:

  • Standard Payout: Within 24-48 hours of confirmed transaction completion
  • Express Payout: Available for verified vendors (additional fees may apply)
  • Holding Period: First-time vendors may have an initial 7-day holding period

Payment Disputes: In the event of a customer dispute, funds may be held until the dispute is resolved. Both parties will be notified of any holds or adjustments.

Minimum Payout Amount: The minimum payout amount is 50,000 SLL. Funds below this threshold will roll over to the next payout cycle.

4. Commissions & Fees

Bayna reserves the right to charge commissions or subscription fees as outlined in your specific vendor tier. Current fee structures include:

Commission-Based Model:

  • Products: 10% commission on each successful sale
  • Services: 12% commission on each completed booking
  • Premium Listings: Optional fee for featured placement (starting at 20,000 SLL/month)

Subscription-Based Model (Coming Soon):

  • Basic Tier: Free (10% commission)
  • Pro Tier: 50,000 SLL/month (5% commission)
  • Enterprise Tier: Custom pricing (negotiable commission rates)

Fee Changes: Bayna may adjust commission rates or fee structures with 30 days' notice. Continued use of the platform after fee changes constitutes acceptance of the new rates.

5. Vendor Performance Standards

To maintain a high-quality marketplace, vendors must meet the following performance standards:

  • Response Rate: ≥ 90% response to customer inquiries within 24 hours
  • Fulfillment Rate: ≥ 95% of orders fulfilled on time
  • Customer Rating: Maintain average rating of 4.0 stars or higher
  • Dispute Rate: Less than 5% of transactions resulting in disputes

Vendors failing to meet these standards may be subject to:

  • Account review and warning
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent removal from the platform
  • Forfeiture of pending payouts (in cases of fraud)

6. Product and Service Quality

Vendors are responsible for ensuring all products and services listed on Bayna meet the following quality standards:

  • Products must be genuine, authentic, and as described
  • Services must be performed professionally and to customer satisfaction
  • All listings must include accurate, high-quality images
  • Prohibited items include counterfeit goods, illegal substances, weapons, and hazardous materials
  • Services must comply with all applicable professional standards and regulations

Quality Guarantee: Bayna may remove listings that receive repeated negative feedback or fail to meet quality standards. Repeat offenders will have their vendor status revoked.

7. Customer Communication

Vendors must maintain professional and respectful communication with customers at all times. Guidelines include:

  • Respond to inquiries within 24 hours
  • Provide accurate and helpful information
  • Do not harass, intimidate, or abuse customers
  • Do not solicit customers to complete transactions outside the platform
  • Honor all commitments and promises made to customers

Off-Platform Transactions: Soliciting customers to complete transactions outside Bayna is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account termination.

8. Prohibited Activities

Vendors shall not engage in the following prohibited activities:

  • Listing counterfeit, stolen, or illegal items
  • Creating fake accounts to manipulate ratings or reviews
  • Price fixing or collusion with other vendors
  • Money laundering or fraudulent transactions
  • Violating intellectual property rights of others
  • Posting misleading or deceptive content
  • Attempting to access other vendor accounts

9. Intellectual Property

Vendors retain ownership of their product images, descriptions, and branding. However, by listing on Bayna, you grant us a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to display your content on the platform.

Vendors must not use Bayna's trademarks, logos, or branding without explicit written permission.

10. Termination of Vendor Status

Voluntary Termination: Vendors may terminate this Agreement at any time by closing their vendor account. All pending orders must be fulfilled before closure.

Involuntary Termination: Bayna may terminate vendor status immediately for:

  • Violation of this Agreement or platform policies
  • Fraudulent or illegal activities
  • Repeated poor performance or negative customer feedback
  • Abuse of customers or other vendors
  • Any conduct harmful to the platform or community

Appeals Process: Terminated vendors may appeal the decision by contacting vendors@bayna.com within 14 days of termination.

11. Dispute Resolution

Customer disputes will be handled through Bayna's dispute resolution process. Vendors agree to cooperate fully with investigations. Steps include:

  • Customer submits dispute with supporting evidence
  • Vendor is notified and given 48 hours to respond
  • Bayna reviews evidence from both parties
  • Decision is rendered within 5-7 business days
  • Either party may appeal the decision

If a dispute cannot be resolved through this process, either party may seek resolution through the courts of Sierra Leone.

12. Liability and Insurance

Vendors are solely responsible for their products, services, and business operations. Bayna is not liable for any damages arising from vendor-customer transactions, including but not limited to:

  • Product defects or service failures
  • Delivery delays or failures
  • Customer injuries or property damage
  • Loss of profits or business opportunities

Vendors are strongly encouraged to obtain appropriate business insurance, including liability insurance, to protect against potential claims.

13. Modifications to Agreement

Bayna may modify this Agreement with 30 days' written notice (email or in-app notification). Continued operation as a vendor after changes constitutes acceptance of the modified terms. Vendors may terminate this Agreement if they disagree with modifications.

14. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Sierra Leone. Any legal proceedings must be filed in the courts of Freetown, Sierra Leone.

15. Entire Agreement

This Agreement, together with the Bayna Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, constitutes the entire agreement between you and Bayna regarding your vendor status, superseding any prior agreements or understandings.
