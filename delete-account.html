Delete Your Account

Bayna (the "App") is committed to giving you control over your personal data. This page provides detailed instructions on how to permanently delete your account and associated information directly from the mobile application.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All your personal data, listings, reviews, and transaction records (except those required by law) will be removed within 30 days. Please ensure you withdraw any pending funds or complete active orders before proceeding.

Step-by-Step Account Deletion Guide

Follow the steps below to permanently delete your Bayna account. The process includes multiple confirmation screens to prevent accidental deletion.

STEP 1
1 Open the Bayna App

Launch the app on your mobile device and ensure you are logged in to the account you wish to delete.

STEP 2
2 Tap the Hamburger / Drawer Menu

Tap the ☰ (hamburger menu) icon located at the top left corner of the home screen. This will open the navigation drawer.

📱 Visual clue: The menu icon looks like three horizontal lines stacked vertically.

STEP 3
3 Tap "Profile" at the Bottom Left

In the open drawer menu, scroll to the bottom left section and tap on "Profile" or your avatar icon to access your personal profile page.

STEP 4
4 Enter Account Settings

On your profile screen, look for the "Account Settings" option (usually represented by a gear ⚙️ icon or labeled text). Tap to proceed.

STEP 5
5 Navigate to Danger Zone

Scroll down within Account Settings until you find a red-bordered or highlighted section labeled "Danger Zone". Inside, tap "Delete Account".

⚠️ The Danger Zone contains sensitive actions. Only proceed if you are certain about deleting your account.

STEP 6
6 Proceed to Delete Account Page

You will be redirected to the Delete Account page. On this page, you will see a detailed warning explaining the consequences of deletion.

Tap the "Proceed to Delete" button to continue.

STEP 7
7 Confirmation Screen & Identity Verification

A final Delete Confirmation Screen will appear. You must complete all three fields to enable the deletion button:

  • Enter your password – To verify it's really you.
  • Reason for deletion (optional) – You may optionally provide feedback to help us improve.
  • Type "DELETE" – In the text box, you must type the word DELETE in uppercase letters exactly as shown.

Once you have entered your password, typed "DELETE", and optionally provided a reason, the "Permanently Delete Account" button will become active (enabled).

STEP 8
8 Final Action

Tap the "Permanently Delete Account" button. A final pop-up will ask for one last confirmation. Confirm to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

✅ After deletion: You will be automatically logged out. Your account will be queued for permanent deletion within 30 days. You will no longer be able to log in or access past orders, messages, or listings using the deleted account credentials.
